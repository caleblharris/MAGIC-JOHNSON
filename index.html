EARLY LIFE

Magic Johnson Jr. was born Earvin Johnson in Lansing Michigan August 14, 1959

As a kid Johnson loved basketball and idolized players such as Bill Russel and Earl "The Pearl Monroe".

After a high school basketball game Earvin was nicknamed "Magic" by a local writer in which he recorded a triple double in the contest.

Staying close to home Magic decide to pursue basketball and attend Michigan State University.

PROFESSIONAL ACCOMPLISHMENTS

  • NCAA Championship 1979
  • 1st overall selection in 1980 NBA draft
  • 5x NBA Champion
  • 3x NBA Most Valuable Player
  • 2x NBA All Star Game Most Valuable Player
  • 9x All NBA first team selections
  • 12x NBA all star
  • 4x NBA assist leader
  • 2x NBA steals leader
  • Member of the legendary dream team and gold medal recpiant
  • 2002 Hall of Fame inductee

    PERSONAL LIFE

      Johnson has been married to long time love "Cookie" since 1991

      He has three childeren EJ, Elisa and Andre.

      Johnson was diagnosed with HIV in 1991

      ENTREPRENEURSHIP

      In 1991 Johnson founded the Magic Johnson Foundation, an organization that provides funds, education and resources about HIV in community programs.

      In 1995 Johnson founded Magic Jonson Enterprises. The investment company has been very successful in distributing revenue in muliple ventures such as The Los Angeles Lakers, movie theaters, sports clubs and resteraunts including TGI Fridays and Burger King.

      In 2001 Johnson formed Johnson's Urban Investments that is dedicated to community development in low income areas.

      In 2012 Johnson and a group of investors bought the Los Angeles Dodgers baseball team

      LEGACY

      One of the greatest NBA players ever, Magic Johnson revolutionized the
      game of basketball with his creativiy, heart and desire. His contributions
      and achievements to the sport will forever be remembered. Johnson's charisma
      and larger than life persona easily made him a well sought after commodity. Off the court,
      Johsnon's philanthropy and entreprenueral spirit has helped so many people and push communities forward.
      A legend in every sense of the word, Magic has left his touch on humanity
      as for him it was not enough to just play the game of life, but change it forever!
